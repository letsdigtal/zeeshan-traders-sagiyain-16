Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to the most common queries from our clients.

How long does a typical custom furniture project take?

Project timelines depend on complexity, but most custom builds take between four to six weeks. We provide a detailed schedule once we review your requirements.

Do you offer on-site consultations?

Yes. We start with a virtual or phone consultation, followed by an on-site visit to capture precise measurements and finalise specifications.

Which materials do you use?

We work with premium hardwoods, engineered panels, and eco-friendly finishes. Material recommendations are tailored to your project’s durability and aesthetic needs.

Can you restore antique furniture?

Absolutely. We carefully assess each piece, source compatible materials, and restore it while preserving its original character.
